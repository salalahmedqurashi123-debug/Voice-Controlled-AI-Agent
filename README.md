🏆 Award-Winning AI Voice Assistant | STEAM 2024
1st Prize Winner at Hyderabad Level - Govt. College for IT Qasimabad 

Proudly developed representing **Govt. College for IT Qasimabad for the official STEAM 2024 exhibition organized under the Sindh Government.

This project is a highly interactive, voice-controlled AI chatbot leveraging the Google Gemini API and FastAPI. It is designed to understand natural language, perform automated web tasks, and provide seamless text-to-speech responses.

✨ Core Features:**
 🎙️ **Real-Time Voice Recognition:** Accurately captures and processes spoken commands.
 🧠 **Intelligent Responses:** Powered by Google Gemini 1.5 Flash for concise and smart answers.
 🤖 **Web & System Automation:** Automatically searches YouTube/Google and controls cursor movements.
 🔊 **Text-to-Speech (TTS):** Natural sounding voice feedback using pyttsx3.
